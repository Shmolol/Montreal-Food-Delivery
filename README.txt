# SMD Delivery
An eCommerce Food Delivery Service Website 

## Introduction
SMD Delivery is a website designed to offer a convenient platform for ordering food online. It provides users with a selection of food items, including burgers, sides, entrees, and drinks, allowing them to add items to their cart and proceed to checkout for delivery.

## Key Features
Menu Selection: Users can browse through a variety of food items categorized into different sections.

Cart Functionality: Users can add items to their cart and view the total cost of their order.

Checkout Process: Users can enter their information and complete their order through the checkout page.

Responsive Design: The website is designed to be responsive and accessible across different devices and screen sizes.

## Usage
Browsing Menu: Explore the menu sections to view available food items.

Adding Items to Cart: Click on the "Add to Cart" button next to each item to add it to your cart.

Viewing Cart: Click on the "Cart" link in the navigation bar to view your cart and proceed to checkout.

Completing Order: Fill out the required information on the checkout page and click "Place Order" to complete your order.
